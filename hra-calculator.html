How is HRA Exemption Calculated?

The HRA exemption under Section 10(13A) is the minimum of these three amounts:

1. Actual HRA received
2. Rent paid – 10% of (Basic + DA)
3. 50% of (Basic + DA) for Metro | 40% for Non-Metro

Metro Cities for HRA

Only 4 cities qualify as "metro" for HRA: Delhi, Mumbai, Chennai, and Kolkata. All other cities (Bangalore, Hyderabad, Pune, Ahmedabad etc.) are classified as non-metro (40% of Basic+DA).

Documents Required

  • Rent receipts (mandatory if rent > ₹3,000/month)
  • Rent agreement
  • If annual rent > ₹1 lakh: landlord's PAN mandatory
