CTC vs Gross Salary vs Net Salary

  • CTC (Cost to Company): Total amount employer spends on you annually, including PF contributions, gratuity, insurance, etc.
  • Gross Salary: CTC minus employer PF and gratuity = actual salary before deductions
  • Net / In-Hand Salary: Gross salary minus employee PF, professional tax, TDS income tax = money in your bank
