What is PPF (Public Provident Fund)?

PPF is a government-backed long-term investment scheme in India with a lock-in period of 15 years. It offers triple tax exemption (EEE status): contributions are tax deductible under Section 80C (up to ₹1.5 lakh), interest earned is tax-free, and maturity amount is completely tax-free.

PPF Key Facts 2025

  • Current Rate: 7.1% per annum (compounded annually)
  • Min Deposit: ₹500 per year | Max Deposit: ₹1,50,000 per year
  • Lock-in Period: 15 years (extendable in blocks of 5 years)
  • Partial Withdrawal: Allowed from 7th year onwards (up to 50% of balance)
  • Loan: Available from 3rd to 6th year (up to 25% of 2nd preceding year balance)
  • Tax Benefit: EEE — Exempt-Exempt-Exempt (most tax-efficient investment)
