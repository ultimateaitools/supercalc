Credit Card Tips to Avoid Interest

  • Always pay full dues by due date to avoid interest charges
  • Credit cards have interest-free period of 18-55 days only if full amount is paid
  • Minimum payment traps you — even ₹50K could take 10+ years with minimum payment!
  • Consider converting large purchases to 0% EMI at point of sale
  • If already in debt, get a personal loan at 12-15% to pay off 42% credit card debt
