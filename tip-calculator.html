Tipping by Service Type (USA)

  • Restaurant servers: 15-20%
  • Bartenders: $1-2 per drink or 15-20%
  • Hotel housekeeping: $2-5 per night
  • Taxi/Rideshare: 15-20%
  • Hair salon: 15-20%
  • Food delivery: 10-15% (min $2-3)

FAQ

Yes, 20% has become the new standard tip in the USA for good restaurant service. It used to be 15%, but with inflation and the service industry's reliance on tips, 20% is now widely considered the baseline for good service.
Traditionally, tips are calculated on the pre-tax amount. However, many people now calculate the tip on the total bill including tax, which is simpler and slightly more generous to servers. Both are acceptable.
Tipping on takeout is optional but appreciated. A tip of 10-15% for takeout orders is generous. For complex orders or during busy times, consider tipping higher. For coffee shops or quick service, $1-2 is standard.
