Health Benefits of 10,000 Steps/Day

  • Burns approximately 300-500 calories (varies by weight and pace)
  • Covers roughly 7-8 km of distance
  • Takes about 90-100 minutes at normal pace
  • WHO recommends 150 minutes of moderate activity per week — 10,000 steps/day fulfills this
  • Reduces risk of heart disease, type 2 diabetes, and depression
