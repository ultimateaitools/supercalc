Tips to Reduce Electricity Bill

  • Switch to LED: LED bulbs use 80% less energy than incandescent bulbs
  • 5-Star rated AC: A 5-star AC uses 30-40% less power than 3-star
  • Solar panels: 1 kW solar panel generates ~4 units/day, saving ₹1,000-2,000/month
  • Peak hours: Avoid heavy usage between 6-10 PM (peak demand period)
  • Inverter appliances: Inverter-type AC, fridge save 20-40% more energy
