How Banks Calculate Loan Eligibility

Banks use the FOIR (Fixed Obligation to Income Ratio) method. Most banks allow maximum 50-55% of your net monthly income to go towards all EMIs combined (existing + new loan).

Available EMI = Net Income × FOIR% – Existing EMIs
Max Loan = Available EMI × [(1+r)^n – 1] / [r × (1+r)^n]
