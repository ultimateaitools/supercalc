Frequently Asked Questions

New regime is better if your deductions are less than ~₹3.75 lakh. Old regime is better if you claim HRA, home loan interest, 80C (₹1.5L), 80D, etc. Use our "Compare Both" feature to find which saves more for your specific situation.
Under the new tax regime for FY 2024-25, if your net taxable income is up to ₹7 lakh, you get a full tax rebate under Section 87A. This means zero tax if income ≤ ₹7 lakh. Under old regime, the rebate applies up to ₹5 lakh income.
Education Cess is an additional 4% charged on the total income tax computed. It goes towards funding education and health initiatives. For example, if your tax is ₹10,000, cess = ₹400, making total tax ₹10,400.
