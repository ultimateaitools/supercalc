Profit vs Markup vs Margin

Profit = SP – CP
Profit % = (Profit / CP) × 100
Gross Margin % = (Profit / SP) × 100
Markup % = (Profit / CP) × 100 (same as profit %)

Break-Even Units = Fixed Costs / (SP – Variable Cost per unit)

Key distinction: Markup is calculated on cost price, while Margin is calculated on selling price. A 30% markup equals ~23% margin.
