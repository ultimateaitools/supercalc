How Much Water Should You Drink Per Day?

The general guideline is 35–40 ml per kg of body weight per day. However, this varies based on activity level, climate, health status, and individual factors. The old "8 glasses a day" rule is a simplification — your actual needs are personalized.

Signs of Dehydration

  • 🟡 Dark yellow urine — aim for pale straw color
  • 😫 Fatigue & headaches — even 2% dehydration affects brain function
  • 🤢 Dry mouth, dizziness — moderate dehydration symptoms
  • ❤️ Increased heart rate — severe dehydration sign

Water Intake Tips

  • Drink 1-2 glasses of water immediately after waking up
  • Drink 500ml 30 minutes before each meal for better digestion
  • Set hourly reminders on your phone
  • Add lemon/cucumber for taste if plain water is boring
  • Coffee/tea count toward water intake (contrary to old beliefs)
