Home Loan Tax Benefits 2025

  • Section 24(b): Up to ₹2 lakh deduction on interest paid for self-occupied property (old regime only)
  • Section 80C: Up to ₹1.5 lakh deduction on principal repayment
  • Section 80EEA: Additional ₹1.5 lakh interest deduction for first-time buyers (affordable housing, stamp duty value ≤₹45 lakh)
  • Note: Most home loan tax benefits are not available under the New Tax Regime (2024-25 default)
