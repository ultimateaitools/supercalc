What is BMR?

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body burns at complete rest to maintain vital functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production. It represents the minimum energy needed to survive.

BMR Formulas

Mifflin-St Jeor (most accurate):
Men: BMR = 10W + 6.25H – 5A + 5
Women: BMR = 10W + 6.25H – 5A – 161

W = weight(kg), H = height(cm), A = age

TDEE & Calorie Goals

  • TDEE = BMR × Activity Factor — your daily maintenance calories
  • Weight loss: TDEE – 500 kcal/day = ~0.5kg/week loss
  • Weight gain: TDEE + 300-500 kcal/day for lean muscle gain
  • Fat loss: TDEE – 20% for sustainable fat loss without muscle loss
