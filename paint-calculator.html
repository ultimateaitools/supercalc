How Paint is Calculated

Paint (L) = Paintable Area (m²) × Coats / Coverage (m²/L)

Coverage by Paint Type

  • Interior Emulsion: ~130 sq.ft per litre (12-14 m²/L)
  • Exterior Paint: ~100-110 sq.ft per litre (10 m²/L)
  • Primer: 100-130 sq.ft per litre (1 coat essential)
  • Distemper: 160-180 sq.ft per litre (low cost, not washable)

Pro tip: Always buy 10-15% extra paint to account for spillage, touch-ups, and batch variations.
