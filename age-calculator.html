How to Calculate Age?

Age is calculated as the difference between the current date and the date of birth. Our calculator accounts for leap years and different month lengths to give you the exact age in years, months, and days.

Age Calculation Formula

Age = Current Date − Date of Birth

Exact Age = Years + Months + Days (accounting for leap years)

Uses of Age Calculator

  • Government documents & applications require exact age
  • Medical age calculations for treatments & vaccines
  • School/college admissions age verification
  • Retirement planning calculations
  • Passport & visa age requirements
