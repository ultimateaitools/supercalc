Gratuity Formula (Payment of Gratuity Act 1972)

For Covered Employees:
Gratuity = (Last drawn salary × 15 × Years of service) / 26

For Not-Covered Employees:
Gratuity = (Last drawn salary × 15 × Years of service) / 30

Key Rules

  • Eligibility: Minimum 5 years of continuous service (4 years 240 days in some rulings)
  • Tax Exemption: ₹20 lakh tax-free for private sector employees
  • Government Employees: Fully tax-exempt without limit
  • 26 days: Monthly days used (excluding 4 Sundays) for "covered" calculation
  • Rounds up: Service of ≥6 months in last year rounds up to full year
  • 10+ employees: Companies with 10+ employees must pay gratuity
