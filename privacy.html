🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 23, 2025

1. Information We Collect

supercalc.online is a free tool website. We collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, browser type, device type via Google Analytics
  • Calculator Inputs: All calculations are performed locally in your browser. We do NOT store or transmit any numbers you enter.
  • Cookies: We use cookies for theme preference (dark/light mode) and Google AdSense advertising.

2. How We Use Information

  • To analyze website usage and improve our calculators
  • To display relevant advertisements via Google AdSense
  • To save your preferences (theme mode)

3. Google AdSense

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google uses cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects anonymous usage data. You can opt out by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Your Calculator Data

Important: All calculations are performed entirely in your browser using JavaScript. We never send your calculation inputs (loan amounts, personal health data, financial figures) to our servers. Your data stays on your device.

6. Cookies

We use the following cookies:

  • SuperCalc-theme: Stores your dark/light mode preference (localStorage)
  • _ga, _gid: Google Analytics cookies for usage tracking
  • Google AdSense cookies: For advertisement personalization

7. Children's Privacy

SuperCalc is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
