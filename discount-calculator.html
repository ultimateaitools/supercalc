How to Calculate Discount?

Sale Price = Original Price × (1 – Discount%/100)
Savings = Original Price × Discount%/100
Discount% = (Savings / Original Price) × 100

Real Life Examples

  • ₹2000 item at 30% off → 2000×0.70 = ₹1400 (save ₹600)
  • Item bought at $80, original $100 → Discount = (20/100)×100 = 20% off
  • After 20% off + 10% additional → effective = 100×0.80×0.90 = 28% off (not 30%!)
